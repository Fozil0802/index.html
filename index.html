2.Brad Pitt



William Bradley Pitt (born December 18, 1963) is an American actor and film producer. He is the recipient of various accolades, including two Academy Awards, two British Academy Film Awards, two Golden Globe Awards, and a Primetime Emmy Award. As a public figure, Pitt has been cited as one of the most powerful and influential people in the American entertainment industry.

3.Dwayne Johnson



Dwayne Douglas Johnson (talaffuzi: Dueyn Duglas Jonson; 1972-yil 2-mayda tugʻilgan), The Rock nomi bilan ham tanilgan, amerikalik aktyor, kinoprodyuser va sobiq professional kurashchidir[2][3][4]. Johnson barcha davrlarning eng buyuk professional kurashchilaridan biri sifatida eʼtirof etiladi. U aktyorlik boʻyicha faoliyatini davom ettirishdan oldin sakkiz yil davomida Jahon kurash federatsiyasida kurashchi boʻlib ishlagan. U suratga tushgan filmlar shu kungacha Shimoliy Amerikada $3,5 milliard, dunyo boʻylab esa $10,5 milliarddan[5] koʻproq daromad koʻrgan.

4.Tom Cruise
